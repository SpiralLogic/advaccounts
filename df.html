TRANSPONDERS - Opening Cars with Electronic Immobiliser or Security Systems:

Most makes and models of vehicles manufactured after 1995 are fitted with an immobiliser / transponder security system. A transponder security system consists of an electronic chip placed in the head of the key which is recognised by the vehicle's computer when the key is turned on. The computer sends a signal to the transponder chip via an aerial and if the transponder is programmed correctly, the vehicle will start.
